our story

Hi! I'm Jillian — and I make things by hand because I believe the things we create can connect us to something bigger. I'm a scholar at the Philippine Science High School, which means I've been given a lot — and I want to give some of that back. Next year, as part of our LEAP (Leadership and Advocacy Program) activity, I'll be hosting a community event for kids in our local area: offering them lunch, painting with them, and showing them that someone out there is rooting for them. Every piece you buy from Interlaced by Jill helps make that happen.
